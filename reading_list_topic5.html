BEAM101 · Topic 5 · Reading List

Governance Factors in ESG Investing

The papers behind the lecture — corporate governance as the transmission mechanism for ESG.
Readings are drawn from the leading economics and finance journals; foundational books and the legal scholarship on dual-class sunsets are included where they are the original source. Core readings are the empirical spine of the lecture; further readings deepen individual topics. Tags: free open / working-paper version available · journal via the library · book.

Core readings
Start here

Twelve papers: the eight empirical results the lecture is built on, plus four foundational sources.

  1. Adams, R. B. & Ferreira, D. (2009). Women in the Boardroom and Their Impact on Governance and Performance. Journal of Financial Economics 94(2). journalfind it · board diversity & the sign-flip
  2. Malenko, N. & Shen, Y. (2016). The Role of Proxy Advisory Firms: Evidence from a Regression-Discontinuity Design. Review of Financial Studies 29(12). journalfind it · proxy-advisor power (~25pp)
  3. Michaely, R., Ordóñez-Calafí, G. & Rubio, S. (2024). Mutual Funds' Strategic Voting on Environmental and Social Issues. Review of Finance 28(5). freeECGI working paper · greenwashing in the vote
  4. Li, S. Z., Maug, E. & Schwartz-Ziv, M. (2022). When Shareholders Disagree: Trading after Shareholder Meetings. Review of Financial Studies 35(4). journalfind it · the endogenous electorate (data)
  5. Levit, D., Malenko, N. & Maug, E. (2024). Trading and Shareholder Democracy. Journal of Finance 79(1). freeSSRN · the electorate is endogenous (theory)
  6. Gompers, P. A., Ishii, J. & Metrick, A. (2010). Extreme Governance: An Analysis of Dual-Class Firms in the United States. Review of Financial Studies 23(3). freeNBER w10240 · incentive vs entrenchment
  7. Aggarwal, D., Eldar, O., Hochberg, Y. V. & Litov, L. P. (2022). The Rise of Dual-Class Stock IPOs. Journal of Financial Economics 144(1). freeNBER w28609 · the dual-class boom & the wedge
  8. Levit, D., Malenko, N. & Maug, E. (2026). The Voting Premium. Journal of Finance 81(3). freeNBER w31892 · what is a vote worth?
  9. Appel, I. R., Gormley, T. A. & Keim, D. B. (2016). Passive Investors, Not Passive Owners. Journal of Financial Economics 121(1). journalfind it · the Big Three as stewards
  10. Shleifer, A. & Vishny, R. W. (1997). A Survey of Corporate Governance. Journal of Finance 52(2). journalfind it · the big-picture survey
  11. Jensen, M. C. & Meckling, W. H. (1976). Theory of the Firm: Managerial Behavior, Agency Costs and Ownership Structure. Journal of Financial Economics 3(4). freefind it · agency costs
  12. Berle, A. A. & Means, G. C. (1932). The Modern Corporation and Private Property. bookfoundational · ownership ≠ control
